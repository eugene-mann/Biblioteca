About — Issue No. 01

Your Personal Reading Curator

A web app that helps you organize your books, enrich them with metadata, display them beautifully, and discover what to read next via an LLM-powered recommendation engine.

EM
Eugene Mann Creator & Maintainer
“Your bookshelf is a portrait of your mind — a curated gallery of the ideas that shaped you.”

Core Surfaces

Four Features

Feature 01

Smart Ingestion

Search via Google Books API, import from Kindle, or upload a CSV. Multiple paths into your library, all enriched with metadata automatically.

Feature 02

Gallery Library

A grid of covers organized into collections you define. Filter, sort, and search instantly. Every book exactly where you left it.

Feature 03

Deep Book Detail

Full metadata, key quotes, and AI-enriched insights. Rate books, track your reading status, and link directly to purchase.

Feature 04

LLM Discovery

An AI recommendation engine that learns your taste. Steer by topic, explore curated lists, and find your next obsession with Claude.
