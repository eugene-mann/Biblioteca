Biblioteca

A Curator’s Note

March 2026

Dear reader,

I built Biblioteca because I believe your bookshelf is a portrait of your mind — a curated gallery of the ideas that shaped you. And yet, most reading tools treat books like inventory. A title. A date. A checkbox.

Where’s the depth? Where’s the beauty? Where’s the thrill of discovering your next obsession?

Biblioteca is a personal reading curator — a web app that helps you organize your books, enrich them with metadata and meaning, display them in a way that feels like browsing a beautiful bookshop, and discover what to read next through an AI recommendation engine that actually understands your taste.

It’s built for readers who want beauty, discovery, and meaning in equal measure.

With care,

Eugene Mann

What Biblioteca Offers

i.

Smart Ingestion

Add books via Google Books search, import your Kindle library, or upload a CSV. Every path into your library is enriched with metadata automatically.

ii.

A Gallery, Not a List

Your library displayed as a grid of covers, organized into collections you define. Filter by status, sort by rating, search instantly.

iii.

Deep Book Detail

Full metadata, key quotes, and insights enriched automatically. Rate, track status, and link directly to purchase. The complete picture of every read.

iv.

AI-Powered Discovery

An LLM recommendation engine that knows your taste. Steer by topic, explore curated reading lists, and find your next obsession.

Version 1 — Now

Personal Curator

Beauty, discovery, and depth in equal measure. Smart search, a gallery display that makes browsing a pleasure, and LLM-powered recommendations.

Version 2 — Next

Social Library

Share libraries, see what friends are reading, recommend books to each other, and build a bookshelf view that feels like visiting a friend’s home.

Colophon

Built with Next.js and React, styled with Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui, powered by Supabase, enriched by the Google Books API, guided by Claude, and hosted on Vercel.
